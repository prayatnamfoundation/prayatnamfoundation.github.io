What We Are

The Prayatnam Foundation welcomes you in our official website. The only goal is to make our society better as before.

The foundation bas formed back in 2020 for helping  needy people   suffering from that  deadly corona virus  around us . From 2020 to 2021 our team has done many social  welfare works that has motivated  and has helped more than 1800  people and during that  team  have also feeded street animals and cows.

What We Do

Prayatnam Foundation always have a mindset to improve the society as much as it can. So we generally pay more attention on those tasks which can add some more values in the society. Tasks achieved till now • Tried our best to help people during Covid-19 Pandemic • Distributed winter clothes • Fed food to needy people • Providing better education to poor children And always looking to bring a change in the society.
